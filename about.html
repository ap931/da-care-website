Origin Story

Da.care was born when Marina and Philip met and began together their journey through body, mind, and soul. What they discovered, they felt called to share—and to create a space where others could share their discoveries too. A place of mutual awakening.

The name stands for “the” and “yes” care, influenced by the Swiss/British (Philip) and Bulgarian (Marina) roots. The company is based in Switzerland, yet our team is international. We also have offices in Sofia, Bulgaria and soon in Javea, Spain.

Working with da.care you can expect:

Young Talents

Our internships aren't about coffee runs—you'll work on real projects that matter. Many lead to permanent positions, and we're flexible enough to accommodate your studies. Start meaningful work now, not after graduation.

Experienced Professionals

We offer a flowing career path where you can grow authentically, with the flexibility to work where and when it suits you (we encourage face-to-face time, but trust you to find the balance). Parents get extra flexibility, pets are welcome, and we provide practical support that helps you live better—from mortgage assistance to childcare and medical coverage.

Seniors

Your decades of wisdom matter here. We offer flexible arrangements that fit your life, opportunities to mentor the next generation, and part-time or project-based work if you prefer. This is a place where experience isn't just respected—it's celebrated and shared.

Team Spirit

Working at da.care is being part of a new type of company where profit is not the centre of attention of the team. Our main objectif and how we measure result is on how our community grows and uses our tools to lead a better life.

We are here for one another, for our job openings.
